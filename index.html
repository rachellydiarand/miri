With my background, I have strong opinions about punishment and jails/prisons/mental hospitals. I feel that the punishment of incarceration is a taking away of your time. Your freedom. I'm not into the torture and abuse of it. I like these few bullet point ideas:

1. Solitary is humane.
2. Clothing can be your own things.
3. Do your own laundry like once a month.
4. Once released, you have a clean record as far as the public is concerned. The time you served is the price you paid.

Without solitary, imprisonment can become a trade school for criminals. And a criminal resort hotel. Not at least offering solitary to everyone is simply backwards. It helps the people who are the most deserving of the punishment.
